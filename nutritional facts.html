Beef and potato porridge is a nutritional dish that combines protein, carbohydrates and various vitamines and minerals. Here's a general overview of its nutritional components per serving(about 1 plate):

  • Calories: approximately 200-300 kcal, depending on the amount of beef and potatoes used.
  • Protein: around 15-20 grams, mainly from the beef, which is a good source of high-quality protein.
  • Carbohydrates: about 20-30 grams, mostly from the potatoes, providing energy and fiber.
  • Fat.
  • Vitamins and minerals: potatoes are rich in vitamin C, potassium and vitamin B6. Beef provides iron, zinc and vitamin B12.

The nutritional values can vary based on the recipe, portion sizes and additional ingredients.
